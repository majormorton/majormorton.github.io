Readers’ Theatre

In the beginning was the word and the word spoke and took flight.

Ear to ear, station to station.

I love directing performed readings. They’re like batting practice and a tune-up on the run.

With limited resources of time, the challenge for both actor and director is to quickly understand and bring forward the playwright’s most essential ideas and tones.

I don't like plays but I like these readings.
