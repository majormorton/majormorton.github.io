The "School of Hard Knocks'' is an idiomatic expression that refers to the experience and education gained through life's challenges and hardships rather than formal schooling. It emphasizes practical experience and the lessons learned from real-life situations, often in a tough or unforgiving environment.

If you learn something in the school of hard knocks, you learn it as a result of difficult or unpleasant experiences.
